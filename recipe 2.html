Enchiladas verdes

Enchiladas have a deep history in Mexico. Their is different colored enchiladas depending on what part of Mexico your from. The dish was orignially created by the Aztecs who would fill rolled tortilla with things like beans or fish dipped in chili sauce.

Recipe for Enchiladas

Brief description

Enchiladas are tortillas rolled with things like beans or meat with the tortilla dipped in the sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1.5lbs of tomatillos
  • 1lb of chiles verdes
  • 1 Packet of tortilla
  • Choice of beans or meat
  • 0.5lbs of Cilantro
  • Queso Fresco
  • Lettuce

Directions

  1. Put tomatillos and chile verdes and cilantro in blender
  2. Blend until liquid and not chunky
  3. Roll up tortilla with filling choice
  4. put a layer of the salsa verde onto the pan and make sure to have some remaining salsa
  5. Put rolled tortilla with filling ontop of salsa verde pan
  6. Dip and roll the tortilla with filling in the pan with salsa verde
  7. Warm up stove and fry the rolled tortilla with fillings covered in salsa

Final Result!!

Can vary depending on filling and add ons.
